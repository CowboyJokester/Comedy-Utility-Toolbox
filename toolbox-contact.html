Contact & Feedback

Questions, ideas, suggestions, success stories, and beta tester feedback are always welcome.

Thank you for visiting the Comedy Utility Toolbox (CUT).

CUT continues to grow because of the ideas, questions, suggestions, and encouragement shared by comedians, comedy writers, storytellers, speakers, performers, and students who use it.

Whether you've discovered a typo, found a broken link, have an idea for a future tool, or simply want to share how CUT helped you organize or strengthen your material, I'd genuinely enjoy hearing from you.

Every thoughtful message helps make the Toolbox a little better.

Although I can't promise to implement every suggestion, I do promise to read every thoughtful message and carefully consider every idea.

How You Can Help Improve CUT

Helpful feedback may include:

  • Report a broken link or technical problem.
  • Suggest a new comedy tool or future feature.
  • Share a comedy-writing technique that has worked for you.
  • Tell me how you've used CUT in your writing, performances, speeches, or creative projects.
  • Recommend improvements to existing tools.
  • Point out anything that could be clearer or easier to use.

Success Stories

One of the most rewarding parts of creating the Comedy Utility Toolbox is hearing how people put the tools to work.

If CUT helped you discover a stronger premise, improve a callback, organize a setlist, develop a character, strengthen a speech, or simply think differently about comedy, I'd love to hear your story.

Your experience may help shape future tools and encourage other creators along the way.

A Personal Thank You

I've spent many years performing, studying, teaching, and observing comedy.

Thank you for visiting, for supporting the project, and for helping make CUT better with every suggestion and every conversation.
