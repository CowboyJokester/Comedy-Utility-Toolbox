How To Use The Callback Strength Analyzer

Purpose

This tool helps you judge whether a callback is strong enough to use later in a joke, story, bit, routine, rodeo skit, or full set.

How It Works

  1. Enter the original callback seed.
  2. Enter the planned callback line.
  3. Choose how memorable, surprising, connected, and delayed the callback is.
  4. Click Analyze Callback.

Comedy Tip

A callback works best when the audience remembers the original seed but does not expect it to return.
