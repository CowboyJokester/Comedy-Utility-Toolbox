Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

At the Comedy Utility Toolbox, we believe your ideas, observations, and creative work belong to you. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is handled.

Important:
The Comedy Utility Toolbox does not currently require user accounts, subscriptions, or online storage. Most information created inside the Toolbox is stored locally on your own browser and device.

Information We Collect

At this time, the Comedy Utility Toolbox does not collect personal information simply from using the tools.

If you contact us by email, we may receive information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Comments, feedback, bug reports, or suggestions

Your Comedy Material

Your jokes, ideas, stories, premises, notes, and other creative material belong to you.

The Comedy Utility Toolbox does not claim ownership of any material you create using the tools.

Local Browser Storage

Some tools use your browser's local storage feature to save your projects and preferences. This information remains on your device.

Please understand that clearing your browser data, cache, or local storage may remove saved projects. We recommend maintaining personal backups of important comedy material.

Future Services

As the Comedy Utility Toolbox grows, future versions may include services such as user accounts, cloud saving, premium memberships, or payment systems.

If those services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain how that information is collected and protected.
