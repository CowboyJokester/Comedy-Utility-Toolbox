Truth Detector

Find the honest emotional truth hiding underneath a joke idea, story, complaint, or observation.

Need help finding the truth underneath a joke idea?

How to Use This Tool

Purpose

The Truth Detector helps uncover the honest emotional truth underneath a joke idea, complaint, story, or observation so the material has stronger roots.

When To Use It

  • When a premise feels clever but thin.
  • When a complaint needs a more relatable emotional core.
  • When you want to turn frustration, embarrassment, fear, pride, jealousy, or old-school thinking into joke material.

How To Use It

  1. Enter a premise, observation, complaint, or story idea.
  2. Choose a comedy direction.
  3. Click Detect Truth.
  4. Review the hidden truth, comedy angle, setup, punchline direction, and tag idea.
  5. Use the strongest truth as the foundation for jokes, callbacks, or a longer bit.

Example Input

Premise: I hate self-checkout because it makes me feel like I work at the store.
Direction: Frustrated Truth

Example Output

Hidden Truth: A small inconvenience is making you feel powerless.
Comedy Angle: Treat the machine like a bossy supervisor who forgot to put you on payroll.

Comedy Tip

Audiences laugh at exaggeration, but they connect with truth. Find the truth first; then let the punchlines hitch a ride.
